## Key Insights

1. **Volume Gap**: Competitor keywords have much higher search volume (18,931 avg) compared to Phase 1B (318 avg)
   - Top 3 keywords alone account for 120,500+ monthly searches combined
   - Strong commercial intent across all competitor keywords

2. **Horse Breed Focus**: ehorses.de and pferde.de heavily optimize for specific horse breeds
   - Haflinger, Araber, Friese are major keywords with 18k-27k searches
   - Opportunity: Create breed-specific content pages

3. **Generic vs. Specific**:
   - Generic keywords (pferd, pferde) have highest volume
   - Niche keywords (bayerns pferde, süddeutsches kaltblut) have lower volume but higher relevance

4. **Competitor Strategy**:
   - ehorses.de dominates with generic marketplace terms
   - pferde.de and bayerns-pferde.de target more regional/niche keywords
   - Gap exists for Bayern-specific, local marketplace positioning
