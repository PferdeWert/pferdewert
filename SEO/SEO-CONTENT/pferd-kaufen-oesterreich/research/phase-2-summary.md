## Content Gaps Analysis

### CRITICAL GAPS (High Impact Opportunities)

#### Gap 1: NO Educational Content in Top 10
- **Opportunity:** Create comprehensive buyer's guide
- **Keywords:** "pferd kaufen österreich" (primary), all cluster keywords
- **Reasoning:** 100% of top 10 are transactional (marketplaces). Informational buyers must navigate marketplaces for guidance. This is the #1 content gap.

#### Gap 2: No Pricing Guide Specific to Austria
- **Opportunity:** Create detailed price guide by breed, use case, region, quality
- **Keywords:** Price-related PAA (40% of questions)
- **Reasoning:** PAA shows pricing is dominant buyer concern. Google ranks pricing guides highly. No such guide exists in top 10.

#### Gap 3: No "How to Buy" Process Guide
- **Opportunity:** Step-by-step process from preparation to registration
- **Keywords:** "pferd kaufen österreich" (buying_process intent)
- **Reasoning:** PAA "Kann ich einfach so ein Pferd kaufen?" indicates process confusion. No comprehensive process guides in top 10.

#### Gap 4: No Marketplace/Source Comparison
- **Opportunity:** Compare ehorses vs Willhaben vs Landwirt vs BillyRider vs direct breeders
- **Keywords:** "willhaben pferde" (6600 SV), "landwirt pferde" (3600 SV)
- **Reasoning:** Navigational keywords show buyers seeking marketplace guidance. Comparative content would serve this intent while adding value beyond marketplaces.

#### Gap 5: Limited Austrian Legal/Regulatory Content
- **Opportunity:** Comprehensive guide on Austrian horse ownership requirements by state
- **Keywords:** Regional keywords, "pferd kaufen österreich" (legal angle)
- **Reasoning:** Government sources ranking (noe.gv.at) but sparse. Opportunity for more comprehensive guide with higher SEO authority.

### SECONDARY GAPS (Medium Impact Opportunities)

#### Gap 6: Minimal Breed-Specific Buyer Guides
- **Opportunity:** Noriker (2400 SV) and Haflinger (1300 SV) buying guides
- **Keywords:** "noriker kaufen", "haflinger kaufen"
- **Reasoning:** Phase 1 shows breed keywords have highest volume (3700 SV combined). ehorses only has category pages, no informational guides.

#### Gap 7: No Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) Guide
- **Opportunity:** Complete financial planning guide (purchase + stabling + feed + vet + insurance + equipment)
- **Keywords:** PAA monthly cost question, "pferd kaufen österreich" (financial planning)
- **Reasoning:** PAA shows €500+/month question. Buyers need full financial picture before purchase. TCO guides convert well.

#### Gap 8: Limited Regional Market Analysis
- **Opportunity:** Regional guides for Tirol (+23% growth), Kärnten (+50% growth), Steiermark
- **Keywords:** Regional cluster keywords (1700 SV total)
- **Reasoning:** Phase 1 identifies regional growth trends. ehorses has regional categories but no market analysis guides.

#### Gap 9: Underserved Budget Buyer Segment
- **Opportunity:** Guide for budget-conscious buyers (under €1,000, rescue angles)
- **Keywords:** "pferde kaufen für 200 euro" (0.61 CPC - highest), "pferde günstig kaufen"
- **Reasoning:** Budget keywords have highest CPC despite low volume - indicates commercial value. PAA shows budget segment interest.
