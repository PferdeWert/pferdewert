### H2 Section 8: Kaufuntersuchung (AKU) & Rechtliche Absicherung
**Word Count**: 240 words
**Content Type**: Guide + Legal Framework
**Priority**: HIGH (MAJOR GAP - Only 2/10 coverage)

**H3 Subsections**:
1. **Was ist AKU? Warum ist es wichtig?**
   - Definition: Ankaufsuntersuchung = Veterinary pre-purchase exam
   - Purpose: Buyer protection against hidden health issues
   - Mandatory recommendation for safe purchase
   - Cost: €200-€1,500 depending on depth

2. **Was prüft der Tierarzt?**
   - Lähmheit (Soundness) - Walk, trot, lunge
   - Atemprobleme (Respiratory) - Listen for wheeze
   - Herzgeräusche (Cardiac) - Heart murmur check
   - Zahnstatus (Teeth) - Age verification
   - Erbkrankheiten (Genetic) - Known breed issues
   - X-rays (Optional, adds €300-€500)

3. **AKU Kosten & Optionen**
   - Small AKU: €200-€350 (basic clinical exam)
   - Full AKU: €800-€1,500 (includes X-rays, ultrasound)
   - Recommendation: Full AKU for >€3,000 purchase

4. **Kaufvertrag Essentials**
   - Pony description (Alter, Rasse, Farbe, Markierungen)
   - Seller liability (Gewährleistung - typical 2 weeks for health)
   - Trial period (Probezeit) - 2-4 weeks standard
   - Payment terms (Zahlung nach AKU möglich)
   - AKU contingency clause
   - Ownership transfer documentation

5. **Rechtliche Absicherung**
   - Right to return within trial period
   - Liability for hidden defects (Erbkrankheiten)
   - Written contract mandatory
   - Ownership transfer must be documented
