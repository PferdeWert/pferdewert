   - Isländer (130-145cm, €5,000-€40,000, hardy/smart)
   - Falabella (65-85cm, €2,000-€5,000, miniature/decorative)
   - Table: Breed comparison (size, price, temperament, best for)
   - H3: Each breed gets 50-word description

4. **Kosten komplett: Kaufpreis + Lebenshaltungskosten + Erstausstattung** (300 words)

     - Junge/ungeritten: €600-€3,000

   - **Monatliche Kosten**: €500-€800
     - Futter: €300-€400
     - Einstreu: €50-€100
     - Hufschmied (6-8 Wochen): €15-€20/month durchschnittlich
   - **Jährlich**: €600-€1,000
     - Tierarzt (Routineuntersuchung): €200-€400
     - Versicherung: €180-€480
     - Sonstiges: €200-€300
   - **Total Year 1**: €4,000-€7,000 (Kaufpreis + Ausrüstung + Unterhalt)
   - **Total Year 2+**: €1,200-€2,000/year
   - Table: Budget breakdown by cost category
   - Interactive: Annual cost calculator

   - Pony carries 80-100kg vs. Horse carries 120-150kg
   - Ponys live 30-35 years vs. Horses 25-30 years

     - Cons: No buyer protection

   - Decision matrix: By buyer profile (budget, experience, needs)

     - Seller liability (Gewährleistung)
     - Trial period (Probezeit) - typically 2-4 weeks
     - Payment terms
     - AKU results reference
   - **Legal rights**:
     - Rückgaberecht within trial period
     - Haftung for hidden defects (Erbkrankheiten)
     - Dokumentation of ownership transfer
   - Sample contract template / checklist
